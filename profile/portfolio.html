2010

R4E Project (Rim-Rotor Rotary Ramjet Engine)

Research assistant - CAMUS group

Objective : Produce positive trust at tip speeds over Mach 1, ideally Mach 3.

This research studies a new engine concept based on rotating ramjets. Ramjets are thrust producing supersonic engines with no moving part. In the proposed engine, ramjets are brought to supersonic speeds while being attached to a high speed rim-rotor flywheel.

My contribution to this project was to design, build and operate the test bench specially made for this unique engine... >>

2009 - 2010

Supermileage SAE Project

Technical director, chassis head designer

Objective : Design and built a car with a consumption of 1500 km/L (>3500 mpg)

As an end of degree project, the team of 14 future mechanical engineer has decided to design and build a single-seated vehicle with minimalistic fuel consumption. The major challenge was to upgrade a 3.5hp B&S engine for an efficiency over 25%, and design a complete car under 44 kg.

On this project, I did the carbon chassis design and a part of the engine upgrade and testing in addition to the advisor role ... >>
