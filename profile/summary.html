I am an energetic, skilful and creative mechanical engineer, graduated from a program focussed on product development. I have genuine interests in renewable energy and automotive industry, especially for greener transportation. I also have a fast and accurate understanding of physical phenomena, developed through very diversified work experiences. I am looking forward to help your company developing next-generation environmentally friendly technologies.

Throughout 4 years of engine development and testing, twenty months of internships and 4 years of successful extracurricular projects and, I have developed relevant skills to product design, testing and optimisation. My principal technical skills are in mechanical product design and in engine instrumentation and testing, which includes data analysis and controller calibration know-how.

Hired within the Combustion, Performance and Emissions (CPE) team at Westport Innovation, I have been primarily responsible of engine testing, calibration and verification to meet the products design requirement, including emission regulation. To achieve those requirements, my primary activities were the planning and management of engine tests in a dedicated test cell, the analysis of test data for reporting results and documenting components failure, and finally the calibration of specific engine work packages assigned to me.

I have also designed, build and operated two other engine test benches. One was for supersonic hydrogen powered engine experiment on an R4E, using Labview for controls and data acquisitions and Matlab for data treatment and analysis. I also did FEA and CFD analysis on the structural body of a Supermileage prototype car to achieve the lightest and the most efficient monocoque shell. For the same project, I did thermal studies on the internal combustion spark engine of the car to choose the best suitable materials and the optimum thermodynamic configuration. Those studies were validated with real physical mechanisms on the second test bench I worked on.

I have been many times compared to a cyborg by my colleagues because of my strong computer skills, and abilities to learn quickly. I have learn to work with a large variety of engineering programs from mechanical assisted software (CAD, CFD and FEA) to calibration and analysis tools (matlab/simulink, ATi Vision and CANalyzer). I handle very easily the complete Microsoft Office programs, including Excel macros (Visual Basic), Access and Expression Web (HTML5, CSS and Javascript).

In fact I am a modest person, but you need to know that I have always been among the best in what I did. I successfully lead teams of 2 different project (Baja SAE and Supermileage SAE) to obtain the 1st place at each respective international competition involving more than 70 North American universities. Innovation and quality have always been the main focus in every aspects of my work, and I am ready to turn new challenges into great success.
